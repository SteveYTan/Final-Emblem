How to play

If this is your first time playing, Please wait for all the sprites to load, The delay is due to pokemonapi

To play click on one of your pokemon until it has a blue highlight.
It is ready to move when its action bar reaches
full and it will display "Pokemon Is ready" on the bottom of the UI.

To move pokemon simply press the WASD keys while it is highlighted

The number of moves is shown on the UI.
when it runs out it cannot move anymore and its action bar will start counting again

To attack click on a target on middle of UI when a highlighted pokemon is near an enemy pokemon.
Attacking will reset the action bar

Victory is achieved when all Enemy pokemon is eliminated.
